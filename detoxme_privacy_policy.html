1. Overview

DetoxMe does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers, third parties, or advertisers. All data stays on your device.

DetoxMe ("the App") is an Android application developed and maintained by Faazi IoT Solutions. The App helps users monitor and limit their time spent on selected applications. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

By installing and using DetoxMe, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

3. Data we access and store

DetoxMe accesses and stores the following data locally on your device only using an Android Room (SQLite) database. This data never leaves your device.

Data type What it is Why it is stored/accessed
Installed Apps List The list of applications currently installed on your device. To allow you to easily search for and select which specific apps you want to restrict during your detox sessions.
Daily Usage Time The number of minutes/seconds you have used each selected app per day. To display your usage reports and trigger blocks when limits are reached.
Time Limits The daily screen time limits you configure per app. To enforce app blocking when your chosen limit is reached.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, payment details, device identifiers, or any other personal information. We never track what you do inside the apps you are monitoring.

4. Permissions used

DetoxMe requires the following Android permissions to function. Each permission is used solely for core app functionality and your privacy is respected.

Permission Type Why it is needed
Query All Packages (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES) Sensitive Allows DetoxMe to see the list of apps installed on your device. This is necessary so you can choose which apps to monitor and limit.
Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) Sensitive Allows us to securely track your screen time. We use this strictly to calculate how long you have been actively using the apps you chose to limit.
Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) Sensitive Allows our "Block Screen" to instantly pop up over distracting apps the exact moment your configured time limit is reached.
Foreground Service (Special Use) Normal Keeps our tracking timer running accurately in the background without your phone's battery saver shutting it down during an active detox session.
Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) Normal Shows a persistent status bar icon while a detox is active, keeping you informed that your time limits are currently being enforced.
Billing Normal Allows for secure in-app purchases via Google Play if you choose to upgrade to premium features.

Important note on Sensitive Permissions: DetoxMe strictly adheres to Google Play's Developer Policies. The Query All Packages and Usage Access permissions are used entirely locally. We do not transmit your installed app inventory or your app usage habits over the internet.

5. Data sharing and third parties

DetoxMe does not share any data with any third party. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or cloud services integrated into the App.

Specifically, DetoxMe does not:

  • Send any data to external servers or the internet
  • Use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any cloud analytics service
  • Use any advertising network or display ads
  • Integrate any social login or third-party authentication

6. Data retention and deletion

All data stored by DetoxMe resides in a local Room database on your Android device. The data is retained until you:

  • Manually clear the app's data from Android Settings → Apps → DetoxMe → Clear Data
  • Uninstall the App (all app data is deleted by Android automatically)
  • Remove a specific app from monitoring within DetoxMe (deletes that app's local record)

Because we do not have a server, we have no access to your data and cannot delete it on your behalf. You have total control over your data directly on your device.

7. Children's privacy

DetoxMe is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the App stores all data locally and collects no personal information, it poses no specific risk to children. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise app usage on devices used by children.

8. Data security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted over a network, the security of your data is governed by your device's own security measures. We recommend keeping your device updated and secured with a screen lock.
